shameless

2024年06月30日 175 字 大概 1 分钟

Jesus it’s July already

老天，已经七月份了哦

don’t you have any sense of time?

你没有一点时间观念吗?

are you still planning to take examinations?

你还打算考研吗?

so how much have you actually studied?

请问您实际学了多少呢?

okay, imagine the next year

好，你想象一下，明年的这个时候

you see your friends’ admission notes on social media

你在朋友圈里看到你朋友的录取通知书

and you with nothing

而你呢，啥也没有

oh so exciting!

哦~这太刺激了!

so what are you looking at now?

那你现在在看什么?

is there something written on my face?

我脸上有字吗?

you go back to start your fucking books now!

你，现在回去，赶紧开始看书!
